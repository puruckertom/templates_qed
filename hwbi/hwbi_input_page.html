Twenty-two economic, ecosystem, and social service scores were calculated for each county in the conterminous United States. These service scores were then used to calculate eight "domains of well-being", represented as eight sections on the pie chart. The total Human Well-Being Index (HWBI) score is shown in the center of the pie chart.
